Frequently Asked Questions

How does Pawzr work?

Pawzr is an intuitive, user-friendly system for pet owners and service providers. As a pet owner, use Pawzr to find local vet offices, dog parks, and groomers.

Service providers can list their business on Pawzr through our free online directory.

To learn more about how Pawzr works, go here.

Does it cost anything?

Pawzr is absolutely free! Just by signing up, you have instant access to our directory of service providers. You can also create unlimited pet profiles and calendar reminders.

Own a business? Join the online directory to reach a new group of clients -- all through our suite of free online tools.

How is my information shared?

We take your privacy seriously. Because of this, information stored on our site is shared only between you and your service provider.

You may set restrictions on your profile to determine what you want others to see.

For Pawzr Premium, all payments are processed through Stripe, a trusted online resource.

I want to upgrade to Pawzr Premium, what should I expect?

Currently designed for vet offices, groomers, trainers, and dog walkers, Pawzr Premium allows businesses to accept online payments, access and modify employee schedules, and directly message clients and prospects.

Want to learn more? Check out the Pawzr Premium page.

Anything else I should know?

Our goal with Pawzr is to create an unmatched pet community that is both as fun as it is safe and rewarding for our furry friends. We are always working to improve and plan to develop additional services in the months and years to come.

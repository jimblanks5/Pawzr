Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pawzr?

Pawzr is an intuitive, user-friendly system for pet owners and service providers. A software solution made by pet lovers, for pet lovers.

Is it right for me?

Our system is perfect for people who want to simplify their lives. Pawzr makes caring for your pets easy, straightforward, and fast. This allows you to spend more time with the pets you love.

OUR STORY

For many of us, finding a great service provider can be challenging.

We take the guesswork out of it by partnering with groomers, veterinary offices, and pet hotels, all to give you the peace of mind knowing that you are leaving your pet in the hands of someone you can trust.

And with our directory of pet-friendly restaurants and dog parks, you can find the best place to enjoy a day out on the town with your furry friend.  

Our mobile messaging systems allow you to contact businesses with the click of a button.

By using our online booking and payment options, everything can be done from the convenience of your smart device â€“ leaving you more time to spend with the animals you love. 


We even developed a Pet Profile system that allows you to create a digital database for all your pets. You can store their medical history, schedule prescription reminders, and include preferences and photos to send to pet hotels when you travel. 

We wanted to go one step further, though: Trusted doctors and service providers deserve a better way to connect with their clients. So, we designed business solutions to streamline processes, improve efficiency, and help great companies thrive. 

We created Pawzr because we understand the challenges that pet owners and service providers face every day. Our mission is to make life a little easier for us all, so that we can focus on what really matters: the animals in our lives.

Pawzr. Because pets are our world, too.
